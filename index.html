Success is simply a little everyday

Hi everyone! I'm a senior at the Unviversity of Georiga and I enjoy writing in my free time. This website is about my journey on Quora and all of my storeis.

This website will help put all of my answers into one place and allow you to learn a little more about myself.

After writing on Quora for a bit, I realized that I wanted to have a place where I am able to place my answers in a format that I wanted.

I have always wanted to create my own blog or website but I never got around to it until recently.

If you have read through some of my answers then you might notice a common theme of doing a little everyday.

I understand that this is a common cliche that most people know but have you ever gone out of the way to do this?

I always knew this but it wasn't until I started to actually take the time to constantly incoporate this into my everyday life.

I started with simple habits such as reading 10 pages of a book everday or doing 10 pushups a day.

These habits eventually lead me to writing one answer on Quora everyday which is how I started my jounrey on Qoura.

When I first started this habit I did not think too much of it.

I wanted to prove to myself that I could accomplish this goal but I now realize just how rewarding this jounrey has been for me.

I have now completed goals that I never gave a second thought to.
